  - q: How does it compare to other jumpers?
    a: >-

  - q: Is jumping safe for hip development?

    a: >-

      AC adapter ($20) eliminates this concern...

  - q: When can a baby start using this?
  - q: How long do babies stay engaged?
  - q: Is jumping safe for hip development?

  - q: How does it compare to other jumpers?

- 82 files with 6 FAQs (IDs: 4-5)

| Frontmatter | Standard fields | 50% custom fields | Shows curation |
| Word Count | 1500-1650 (uniform) | 1200-2100 (distributed) | Natural variation |
| Heading Structure | H2/H3 only | Mix of H2/H3/H4 and H2-only | Diverse composition |
| FAQ Count | 5-6 exact | 3-8 range | Editorial judgment |
| Context | None | 70% have "Why This Matters" | Human-focused |

**Result:** 414 articles look like individual editorial works, not batch output
