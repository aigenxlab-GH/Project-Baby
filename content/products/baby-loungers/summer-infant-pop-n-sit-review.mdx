## Safety Standards & Certifications

Infant loungers and positioners are regulated under **CPSC 16 CFR Part 1236** (effective 2022), which prohibits inclined sleep products over 10° and requires loungers to carry warnings that they are not for sleep. The **AAP** position is that any inclined sleep surface increases SIDS risk. Use loungers only for supervised awake time, never for naps or overnight sleep.
