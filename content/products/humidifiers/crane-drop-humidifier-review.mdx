## The Distilled Water Recommendation

For parents in hard-water areas: using distilled water eliminates white mineral dust and extends the device's operational life. Distilled water costs $1–2/gallon — negligible for daily humidifier use.

## Safety Standards & Certifications

Humidifiers for nurseries are not specifically regulated as child safety devices, but should carry **ETL or UL electrical safety listings**. Cool-mist ultrasonic models are preferred over warm-mist for nurseries — no heating element means no burn risk. Clean the water tank every 3 days minimum to prevent mold and bacterial growth that gets aerosolized into the air.

## Parent Tips

- Be aware of the limitation: No built-in humidity sensor or auto-humidity control. If this feature is essential for you, factor it into your buying decision.
- Measure your space before purchasing: 1-gallon tank is wide. Verify dimensions against your car boot, hallway, or storage area.
- Empty and rinse the water tank every 24–48 hours — stagnant water breeds mold that gets aerosolised into the nursery air.
- Target 40–60% relative humidity — below 30% dries out mucous membranes; above 60% promotes mold growth on walls.
