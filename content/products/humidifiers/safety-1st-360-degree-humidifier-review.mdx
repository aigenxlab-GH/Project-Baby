## Safety Standards & Certifications

Humidifiers for nurseries are not specifically regulated as child safety devices, but should carry **ETL or UL electrical safety listings**. Cool-mist ultrasonic models are preferred over warm-mist for nurseries — no heating element means no burn risk. Clean the water tank every 3 days minimum to prevent mold and bacterial growth that gets aerosolized into the air.
