The **Graco Slim Snacker High Chair** solves the most common high chair complaint: "it takes over the entire kitchen." The Slim Snacker's 10-inch folded width changes the storage calculus entirely.

## The Slim Fold Feature

Standard high chairs, when folded, still occupy 18–24 inches of width. The Slim Snacker folds to 10 inches — narrower than most doorways. You can slide it behind doors, between the refrigerator and the wall, into closet corners. For apartment dwellers and families with small kitchens, this is a genuinely important feature that justifies choosing it over competitors.

## Six Recline Positions

Most high chairs have 2–3 recline positions. The Slim Snacker has 6, which provides meaningful granularity as your baby develops. Start reclined when introducing purées at 6 months; gradually move to upright as core strength develops.

## Footrest at Three Heights

A footrest that prevents legs from dangling isn't just comfort — it actually helps babies learn to self-feed more effectively. Studies show children eat better when their feet are supported (similar to how adults feel more comfortable eating with feet flat). Three positions accommodate children as they grow taller.

## Dishwasher-Safe Tray

The removable tray insert handles high chairs' most chronic problem: food that bakes onto the tray surface. Pop it in the dishwasher after messy meals and it comes out clean. The outer frame wipes clean easily.
