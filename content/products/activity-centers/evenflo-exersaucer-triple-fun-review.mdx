## Safety Standards & Certifications

Activity centers and exersaucers must comply with **ASTM F2012**, which covers structural integrity, entrapment hazards, and toy component safety. The **AAP recommends limiting jumper/exersaucer use to 20 minutes per session** — extended use in a fixed hip-flexion position can affect developing hip and leg muscle patterns. Always ensure the baby's feet flat on the floor.
