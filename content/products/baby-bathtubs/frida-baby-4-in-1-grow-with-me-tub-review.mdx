## Smart Design Meets Four Growth Stages

The **FridaBaby 4-in-1 Grow-with-Me Baby Bathtub** is a mid-range product that demonstrates FridaBaby's design philosophy: identify specific parent pain points and solve them with thoughtful engineering. At $50, it offers four distinct growth stages (newborn through 24 months), a built-in rinse cup, and a non-slip seat—solving the most common bath-time frustrations parents face.

FridaBaby has built its reputation on products like the Windi (for gas relief) and nose frida (for mucus extraction)—items that solve real, specific problems that parents face. This bathtub follows that same philosophy: it identifies what actually frustrates parents during infant baths and builds solutions directly into the design.

## The Built-In Rinse Cup: One Feature That Matters

The integrated rinse cup is permanently attached to the tub, preventing it from sliding off the edge or getting lost during cleanup—a small detail that disproportionately improves the bathing experience. Sleep-deprived parents appreciate not fumbling for a separate cup mid-bath, especially when your hands are already occupied holding a slippery infant.

During testing, the built-in cup proved genuinely convenient. It's positioned at a natural height and angle for rinsing, and the fact that it can't fall into the water eliminates one minor recurring frustration. This is the kind of thoughtful design that feels obvious in retrospect but that most competitors miss.

## Non-Slip Textured Seat Bottom: Safety Built In

The non-slip textured seat bottom provides meaningful friction against baby movement, ensuring your child remains secure and comfortable throughout the bath, even during the more active phases of development when they're splashing and wiggling. The texture is gentle (not rough) against baby's skin but provides excellent grip.

Babies naturally want to slip and slide during baths—it's part of their developing motor control. A non-slip surface reduces this risk without requiring you to maintain constant grip. For parents with limited hand strength or those managing multiple children simultaneously, this feature is genuinely valuable.

## Four Growth Stages: Beyond Newborn

The four-stage design allows for a seamless transition as your baby grows, adapting to their changing needs and abilities. This adaptability is a significant advantage: it eliminates the need for multiple bathtubs or accessories as your baby develops.

**Stage 1 (Newborn 0–3 months):** Provides a secure and comfortable space for your baby's first baths, with gentle support and firm positioning. The contoured design mimics the security of supported bathing.

**Stage 2 (Infant 3–6 months):** Offers more support and stability as they begin to move and explore. The tub provides adequate space while maintaining security as babies develop stronger movements.

**Stage 3 (Crawler/Sitter 6–18 months):** Accommodates increasing mobility and independence as babies learn to sit up independently. More freedom of movement while maintaining safety through non-slip surfaces.

**Stage 4 (Toddler 18–24 months):** Marks a significant milestone as toddlers learn to sit up and play during bath time. The tub expands functionality to accommodate more interactive bathing.

Most babies outgrow the toddler configuration around 20–24 months as they develop the balance and confidence for full-size tub bathing with non-slip mat support.
