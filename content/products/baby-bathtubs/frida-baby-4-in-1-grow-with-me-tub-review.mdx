## Buying Advice for Baby Bathtubs
When shopping for a baby bathtub, there are several factors to consider to ensure you find the right product for your needs. Some key considerations include:
* **Safety features**: Look for bathtubs with built-in safety features, such as non-slip surfaces or secure drainage systems, to minimize the risk of accidents or injuries.
* **Adaptability**: Consider a bathtub that can grow with your baby, offering multiple stages or configurations to accommodate their changing needs and abilities.
* **Ease of use**: Choose a bathtub that is easy to use, clean, and maintain, with features such as a built-in rinse cup or compact design.
* **Brand reputation**: Research the brand and read reviews to ensure you're purchasing from a reputable manufacturer that prioritizes customer satisfaction and safety.
* **Price**: Set a budget and balance it with your needs and preferences, considering that a higher price may not always guarantee a better product.

Additionally, it's essential to remember that baby bathtubs are not a substitute for adult supervision. **Never leave a baby unattended in any bath product**, as infant drowning can occur in as little as 1–2 inches of water in seconds. Always prioritize your baby's safety and take necessary precautions to ensure a safe and enjoyable bath time experience.
