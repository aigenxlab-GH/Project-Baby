The **Blooming Bath Lotus** has become one of the most photographed baby bath products on Instagram — and it turns out the design is also genuinely useful, not just photogenic.

## How It Works

The Lotus looks like a large, soft flower. Eight petals fold down over the rim of any sink, creating a soft cushioned nest in the basin. Baby lies on the plush surface with the petals cradling them from the sides. The plush absorbs water and keeps baby warm throughout the bath.

## Universal Fit

The fold-over petal design adapts to virtually any sink size — kitchen, bathroom, vessel — unlike rigid foam tubs that must match specific sink dimensions. If you have a sink, you have a Lotus tub.

## Machine Washable Plush

The entire Lotus goes in the washing machine — a genuine advantage over plastic tubs that can harbor bacteria in hard-to-reach corners. Monthly washing keeps the plush fresh and hygienic.

## What Parents Love Most

Beyond the practical benefits, parents love the aesthetic. Bath time photos in the Blooming Bath Lotus are universally charming — baby nestled in a soft flower is an irresistible image. For new parents documenting every moment, this is a real consideration.

## The Drying Caveat

Plush takes longer to dry than foam or plastic. Inadequate drying leads to mildew — the main complaint in negative reviews. A dedicated hook near a window or fan speeds drying significantly.

The **Blooming Bath Lotus** is the right choice when choosing a baby bathtub if:

- The spec that matters most for your situation: **Material: Ultra-soft plush polyester**

**Think twice if:** Takes longer to dry than foam alternatives.

## Safety Standards & Certifications

Infant bath seats and tubs must comply with **ASTM F2670**, which governs stability and structural integrity. **Never leave a baby unattended in any bath product** — infant drowning can occur in as little as 1–2 inches of water in seconds. Bathtub seats are not safety devices and provide no drowning protection.
