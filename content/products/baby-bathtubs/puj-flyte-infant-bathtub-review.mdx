The **Puj Flyte** solves a real problem for urban parents and apartment dwellers: full-size baby bathtubs are bulky, and there's simply nowhere to store them in small bathrooms.

## The Design Breakthrough

The Flyte is made from a flexible foam material that folds completely flat — about 0.5 inches thick — and has a hook hole at the top so it hangs on any towel hook or shower rod when not in use. No dedicated cabinet space required. You can store it behind a door, in a cabinet with the towels, or hanging visibly in the bathroom.

## Sink Bathing Advantages

Bathing a newborn in a sink at counter height is ergonomically superior to kneeling over a floor-level bathtub. Your back stays straight, you have excellent control of the baby, and the sink's natural basin shape contains any splash. The Flyte holds baby in a slight recline while maintaining their head above water.

## Soft Construction

The foam is soft, warm, and BPA-free. Unlike hard plastic tubs, the Flyte flexes to conform slightly to baby's body. The surface is water-resistant and mold-resistant when dried properly.

## The Size Limitation

This is a newborn-to-early-infant product. Once baby can sit independently (around 5–6 months), they've outgrown it. Parents who plan beyond the newborn stage should pair this with a transition to another bath product — the Fisher-Price 4-in-1 or a bath seat works well as a follow-on.
