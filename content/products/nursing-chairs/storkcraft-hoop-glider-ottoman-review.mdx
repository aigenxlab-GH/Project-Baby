## Safe Use & Setup  
**Setup and Safety Tips**  
Proper assembly is crucial for the Storkcraft Hoop Glider and Ottoman. Begin by laying out all components on a flat surface to identify missing parts early. Use the included hex key to secure bolts, tightening them snugly but avoiding over-torquing to prevent stripping. The chair’s wall-friendly glide track is designed to avoid scuffing floors, but ensure the floor is clean and dry to prevent slips. Once assembled, test the motion by gently pushing the chair forward and backward to confirm smooth operation. If the glide squeaks, apply a silicone-based lubricant to the ball bearings—avoid oil-based products, which attract dust.  

For safety, position the chair at least 12 inches away from walls or furniture to allow free movement. Always keep the ottoman within reach to support proper posture; using it without elevating feet can strain the lower back. Never leave the chair unattended if a baby is in your lap, and avoid overloading the weight capacity (250 lbs). Before using, check for loose screws or wobbling, which may indicate improper assembly. For long-term durability, avoid placing the chair in high-traffic areas or exposing it to direct sunlight, which can fade the microfiber fabric.
