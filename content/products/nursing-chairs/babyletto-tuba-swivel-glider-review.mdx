## Safety Standards & Certifications

Nursing gliders and recliners are covered by **BIFMA (Business and Institutional Furniture Manufacturer's Association)** standards for structural integrity and durability. Look for chairs tested to hold **300–400 lbs** to accommodate both parent and baby during feeding. Mechanism durability (glider track, recliner mechanism) is the most common failure point — choose chairs with metal rather than plastic glider mechanisms for longevity.
