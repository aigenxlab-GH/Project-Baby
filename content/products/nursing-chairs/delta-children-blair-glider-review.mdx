The **Delta Children Blair Nursery Glider** boasts a sturdy engineered wood frame, which is a common material used in mid-range nursery furniture. At 33.5 inches wide and 32.5 inches deep, the chair is spacious enough to accommodate most parents, but its back height may be a concern for taller users.

One of the notable features of the chair's design is its tufted back, which adds a touch of sophistication to the overall aesthetic. The grey, beige, or white color options provide a versatile and neutral look that will easily blend in with most nurseries. In terms of durability, the chair's engineered wood frame is designed to withstand the rigors of daily use. ## Buying Guide

When choosing a nursing chair, consider the following factors:

* **Age Range**: Nursing chairs are suitable for parents with babies from 0-12 months. However, some chairs, like the **Delta Children Blair Nursery Glider**, are designed to be used for extended periods, making them suitable for parents with toddlers as well.
* **Safety Tips**: Always ensure the chair meets JPMA certified safety standards. Check the chair's weight capacity and ensure it can accommodate both parent and baby during feeding.
* **Common Mistakes**: Be wary of budget gliders that compromise on safety and quality. Avoid chairs with flimsy construction or poor ergonomics, as they may lead to discomfort and injuries.
* **Considerations**: When choosing a nursing chair, consider the chair's size, weight capacity, and features, such as the glide mechanism and lumbar support. Think about the chair's aesthetic appeal and whether it fits your nursery's decor.

## Safe Use & Setup

To ensure safe use and setup of the **Delta Children Blair Nursery Glider**, follow these guidelines:

* **Read the manual carefully**: Before assembling and using the chair, read the manual carefully to understand the instructions and safety precautions.
* **Assemble the chair correctly**: Follow the manufacturer's instructions for assembling the chair to ensure it is stable and secure.
* **Check the chair's weight capacity**: Ensure the chair can accommodate both parent and baby during feeding, and do not exceed the weight capacity.
* **Keep the chair clean and dry**: Regularly clean and dry the chair to prevent the buildup of bacteria and germs.
* **Avoid over-tightening**: Avoid over-tightening the chair's screws or bolts, as this may damage the chair's frame or other components.
* **Supervise children**: Keep children away from the chair when it is in use, as they may accidentally tip or pull on the chair.
