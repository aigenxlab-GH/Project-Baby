The **Ergobaby Evolve 3-in-1 Bouncer** brings Ergobaby's baby-carrying philosophy to the bouncer category: prioritize natural movement, ergonomic positioning, and longevity over electronic features.

## Three Modes That Justify the Investment

**Mode 1 — Infant Bouncer:** Baby lies in a supported recline, fully cradled. The natural bounce responds to baby's movements, teaching cause-and-effect from the earliest weeks.

**Mode 2 — Supported Sitting:** Around 4–6 months, adjust the seat to a more upright position to support babies who are developing sitting skills. The Evolve provides gentle lateral support without restricting natural movement.

**Mode 3 — Toddler Chair:** Fully upright for toddlers up to 45 lbs — significantly higher than most bouncers. This mode turns the Evolve into a cozy low chair for watching TV, reading, or snacking.

## The Ergobaby Design Philosophy

Ergobaby is best known for baby carriers designed around the "M-position" hip posture. They've applied similar principles to the Evolve: the seat contours to support the spine in a natural position at every age. There are no hard edges, no tight harness points, and no rigid sections that create pressure points.

## Natural vs. Motorized Bounce

The Evolve has no motor, no vibration, no batteries. It bounces when baby moves. Parents expecting the automated rocking of a Fisher-Price or Graco product will find this underwhelming initially. But for many babies, the responsive natural bounce is actually more engaging — it rewards their movements with feedback.
