The **BabyBjörn Bouncer Balance Soft** is the product that parents buy once and keep forever — often passing it to siblings, cousins, or selling it second-hand for nearly retail price years later.

## The Philosophy Behind No Batteries

BabyBjörn's engineers made a deliberate choice: no motors, no vibration. The Balance Soft bounces in response to your baby's own movements — when baby kicks or squirms, the bouncer gently rocks. When baby is still, it settles to rest.

This design teaches babies cause and effect from their earliest weeks. They discover they can control their environment — an important cognitive milestone. It's the same reason BabyBjörn calls it "ergonomically correct stimulation."

## The Ergonomic Design

The seat's distinctive shape supports baby's entire body — head, neck, back, and hips — in a position that follows the natural spinal curvature. This is different from padded bucket seats that can cause slumping. BabyBjörn worked with pediatric orthopedic specialists to create a seat that's comfortable for extended periods without causing postural strain.

## Premium Materials

The soft fabric (available in cotton, cotton/mesh, or 3D mesh) is breathable, gentle against newborn skin, and machine washable. The bouncer's aluminum and polypropylene frame is built to last literally decades — no plastic tabs to break, no foam to compress.

## Converting to Toddler Seat

Around 5–6 months, flip the seat 180 degrees to convert to an upright toddler activity chair. BabyBjörn sells toy bars and activity centers that clip to the frame. This extends the product's life well beyond the typical 4-month bouncer window.

## Safety Standards & Certifications

Infant bouncers and rockers must comply with **ASTM F2167**, which governs structural integrity, restraint system strength, and stability under dynamic loading. The **CPSC** issued guidance in 2019 restricting inclined sleep products to a maximum 10° angle — verify the product meets current standards. Never use a bouncer as a sleep surface.
