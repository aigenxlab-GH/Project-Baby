## Safety Standards & Certifications

Infant bouncers and rockers must comply with **ASTM F2167**, which governs structural integrity, restraint system strength, and stability under dynamic loading. The **CPSC** issued guidance in 2019 restricting inclined sleep products to a maximum 10° angle — verify the product meets current standards. Never use a bouncer as a sleep surface.
