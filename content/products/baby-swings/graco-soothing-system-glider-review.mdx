The **Graco Soothing System Glider** is one of the best-selling baby swings in America — and it's easy to see why. At around $120, it delivers a smooth, calming glide motion that mimics being rocked in a glider chair, without the premium price of smart swings.

## How the Gliding Motion Works

Unlike traditional back-and-forth swings, the Soothing System Glider moves forward and back in a smooth, horizontal plane. Many parents find this motion more natural — it's the same movement you make when sitting in a gliding rocking chair with your baby. Babies who are resistant to arc swings often settle into this one quickly.

## Sound and Soothing Options

The Glider includes 15 songs plus 3 nature sounds (rain, ocean, white noise). Volume adjusts independently of swing speed, so you can run it on high speed with low volume for a fussy daytime nap.

## Build and Setup

Setup takes about 20 minutes with the included instructions. The frame feels sturdy and doesn't wobble. The seat pad removes for machine washing — important for newborn blowouts.

## What We'd Change

There's no app or Bluetooth control, and the single gliding motion means you can't switch things up if your baby gets bored with the motion. The footprint is also fairly wide — budget 3 feet of floor space.

## Bottom Line

For families who want a reliable, affordable swing without the smart-home features, the Graco Soothing System Glider is an excellent choice. It does what matters most: it soothes babies.
