The **Hatch Rest 2nd Gen** is the product we recommend to every new parent before they set up the nursery. It does more than any comparable device, and we've seen it transform sleep for babies and toddlers alike.

## More Than a Sound Machine

The Hatch Rest is three devices in one: a high-quality sound machine, a dimmable night light with millions of color options, and (for toddlers) an OK-to-wake clock that communicates through light color. In a nursery where you're trying to minimize gadgets, this is the most efficient single purchase you can make.

## App Control: The Feature That Changes Everything

The Hatch app (iOS and Android) lets you adjust volume, change sounds, and turn the light on and off without opening the nursery door. This is transformative when you're doing a midnight check and don't want to risk waking a sleeping baby by fumbling for a switch.

## The First Two Years

From birth through about 18 months, most parents use the Hatch primarily as a sound machine and night light. The white noise setting is high-quality — true broadband sound rather than a low-fidelity loop.

## The Toddler Years

After about 18 months, the OK-to-wake feature becomes the star. Program green to appear at 7am, and your toddler learns to wait in their room until the light changes. Pediatric sleep consultants widely recommend this product.

## The Subscription Question

The $4.99/month subscription unlocks scheduling and expanded sounds. We recommend trying the free tier first — most families find it adequate.
