The **LectroFan Classic** has been a favorite among sleep consultants and pediatricians for years, and our testing confirms why: it simply makes the best white noise of any machine in its price range.

## The Science Behind Non-Looping Sound

This is the LectroFan's killer feature. Cheap sound machines play an audio recording on a 30–90 second loop. Your baby's brain (and yours) detects the repeating pattern over time, which can actually interfere with sleep. The LectroFan generates white noise electronically, in real time, with no repeating pattern. The result is more consistent, more natural-sounding masking noise.

## 20 Sound Varieties

Ten white noise variants range from pure white (bright, airy) to pink and brown noise (deeper, warmer tones). Ten fan sounds imitate different types of fans — tabletop, box, industrial. Many adults who use fan sounds for sleep find the LectroFan's fan options more natural than digital white noise.

## Precision Volume Control

40 discrete volume levels sounds like overkill until you're trying to find the exact loudness that keeps a baby asleep in a shared room. The fine-grained control is genuinely useful.

## What It Doesn't Do

No night light. No app control. No toddler clock. If you want those features, get the Hatch Rest. The LectroFan is a purpose-built sound machine, and that focus is its strength.

## Who Should Buy It

Parents who primarily want superior white noise quality, those who already have a night light, anyone on a budget who doesn't need the Hatch's multi-function features, and anyone who travels frequently.

## Safety Standards & Certifications

White noise machines are not regulated as medical devices but must meet **FCC Part 15** for electronic emissions. The **AAP recommends** placing white noise machines at least 7 feet from the baby's sleep space and keeping volume below 50 dB — equivalent to a quiet conversation. Most machines tested at close range exceed safe infant hearing exposure levels when set to maximum volume.

The **LectroFan Classic White Noise Machine** scores 8.5/10 — safety standards compliance is factored into this rating and was verified against current CPSC and ASTM requirements at time of review.
