## No Plastic Contact

This is the primary differentiating feature for health-conscious parents. Stainless steel cannot leach any chemicals regardless of temperature, age, or use frequency. Pour hot, pour cold, leave it in a hot car, run it through the dishwasher — the steel is unchanged.

## Safety Standards & Certifications

Sippy cups and transition cups must be **BPA-free** (banned in children's products under CPSC regulations since 2012) and meet **FDA food-contact safety standards** for materials. Look for **phthalate-free and lead-free** certifications. Stainless steel interiors are the safest option as they do not leach any chemicals regardless of temperature. Avoid honey or juice in sippy cups for infants under 12 months.

For the **Contigo Kids Stainless Steel Sippy Cup** specifically: bPA-free, which is independently verified. Our 8.8/10 score factors in full safety compliance.
