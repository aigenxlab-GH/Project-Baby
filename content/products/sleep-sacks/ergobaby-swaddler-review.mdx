## Introduction to the **Ergobaby Swaddler**
The **Ergobaby Swaddler** is a mid-range sleep sack designed for parents who want a structured swaddle that respects their baby's natural posture. As a product from Ergobaby, a trusted brand in the baby gear industry, it brings the same body-position philosophy behind their award-winning carriers to the swaddle category. This philosophy focuses on allowing babies to hold their bodies in a natural position, which is essential for their comfort and development. In this review, we will delve into the design, performance, pros, and cons of the **Ergobaby Swaddler**, as well as provide buying advice for parents who are looking for a high-quality sleep sack.

## Design and Build of the **Ergobaby Swaddler**
The **Ergobaby Swaddler** is designed with natural positioning in mind. It features arm pouches that allow babies to bend their arms at the elbow, rather than pinning them straight down. This design is inspired by the way babies naturally hold their arms when they are in the womb. The arm pouches are made of soft jersey cotton, which is gentle on babies' skin and provides a comfortable fit. The swaddle body wraps snugly around the torso, providing a sense of security and comfort for the baby. The adjustable swaddle tension is another key feature of the **Ergobaby Swaddler**, as it makes it difficult to over-tighten the swaddle, which can be a common mistake with velcro swaddles.

The **Ergobaby Swaddler** is made of organic jersey cotton, which is a high-quality material that is breathable, soft, and gentle on babies' skin. The material is also durable and can withstand multiple washes, making it a practical choice for parents. The swaddle is available in two sizes, Small (7-12 lbs) and Medium (12-18 lbs), which makes it suitable for babies from birth to around 6 months. The closure system is a combination of velcro and snap, which provides a secure and adjustable fit.
