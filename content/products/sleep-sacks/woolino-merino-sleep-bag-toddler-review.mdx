## Why Bamboo?

Bamboo rayon has been gaining popularity in the baby industry due to its exceptional softness, breathability, and temperature-regulating properties. It's 3x softer than cotton, making it an ideal choice for sensitive skin. Bamboo is also naturally hypoallergenic, resistant to dust mites, and requires less water to produce than cotton. These benefits make it a popular choice among parents who prioritize their baby's comfort and health.

Kyte Baby's use of bamboo rayon in their sleep bags has been independently verified as the softest material in the category. This exceptional softness is a game-changer for babies who are prone to eczema or have sensitive skin. The **Kyte Baby Sleep Bag**'s bamboo fabric is also breathable, allowing for airflow and moisture transfer, which helps regulate baby's body temperature and prevent overheating.

## Buying Advice

When choosing a sleep sack, there are several factors to consider:

* **Fabric quality**: Look for soft, breathable, and temperature-regulating materials like bamboo rayon
* **TOG ratings**: Choose a sleep sack with a suitable TOG rating for your baby's age and room temperature
* **Safety certifications**: Ensure the sleep sack meets ASTM F3186 and CPSC flammability standards
* **Color and style**: Consider a sleep sack with a reversible design or a wide range of colors to suit your style and preference
