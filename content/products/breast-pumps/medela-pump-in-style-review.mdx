The **Medela Pump In Style with MaxFlow** is the breast pump brand that almost every American mother has encountered — in the hospital, through insurance, from friends, or at the pediatrician's office. Medela has dominated the market for 30+ years, and the Pump In Style MaxFlow is their flagship consumer double pump.

## 2-Phase Expression: The Technology That Matters

Medela pioneered 2-Phase Expression in the 1990s based on research on how babies actually nurse. A nursing baby doesn't maintain constant suction — they start with rapid, shallow sucks to trigger letdown, then switch to slower, deeper expression once milk starts flowing. The Pump In Style MaxFlow's automatic phase switching mirrors this pattern, which triggers more efficient letdown and better milk output compared to single-speed pumps.

## PersonalFit Flex: Better Comfort

Traditional breast pump flanges are rigid. The PersonalFit Flex connectors are flexible at the base and move with you — important when you're pumping while sitting at a desk or in a car. Five sizes from 21–36mm are included, covering the majority of body types.

## The Work-Ready Kit

The Pump In Style MaxFlow is the most complete pump kit available: pump, tote bag, cooler bag with ice pack, four collection bottles, and all necessary accessories. Everything you need for a workday of pumping sessions fits in the included tote. This is genuinely important when you're already managing the logistics of returning to work while breastfeeding.

## Safety Standards & Certifications

Breast pumps classified as milk-expression devices are regulated by the **FDA as Class II medical devices**, requiring 510(k) clearance. All parts that contact milk must be **BPA-free** and phthalate-free. Under the Affordable Care Act, most insurance plans are required to cover a breast pump — check your insurer before purchasing.

## Parent Tips

- Before buying, account for this known limitation: **Open system**. Understand whether this affects your specific situation.
- For the slightly louder than spectra issue: most parents find a quiet/off mode during evening hours reduces overstimulation — check the manual for volume controls.
- Flange size matters more than any other pump component — measure nipple diameter and size up by 2–3mm for the correct fit.
- Clean pump parts in hot soapy water after every session — dishwasher cleaning degrades silicone parts faster.
