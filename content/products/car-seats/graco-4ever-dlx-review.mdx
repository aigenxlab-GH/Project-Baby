The **Graco 4Ever DLX** makes a bold promise: one seat from birth through booster age. After testing it with a newborn, a toddler, and a 6-year-old, we found the claim holds up — with one important caveat.

## The Four Modes

**Rear-facing (4–40 lbs):** A comfortable, secure position for infants and young toddlers. The 6-position recline ensures safe installation even in vehicles with sloped rear seats.

**Forward-facing with harness (22–65 lbs):** The Simply Safe Adjust system allows one-hand headrest adjustment without re-threading the harness. A genuine time-saver.

**Highback booster (40–100 lbs):** The belt positioning guides the seat belt correctly. The backrest provides head and neck protection in side-impact events.

**Backless booster (40–120 lbs):** The final stage. Most children reach booster age around 4–5 years and remain here until they're ready for the vehicle belt alone.

## Installation

InRight LATCH is the standout feature for daily use. A single lever click confirms a secure connection — no fumbling with lower anchors. Belt installation in booster mode is equally straightforward.

## The Weight Problem

At 30 lbs, moving the 4Ever DLX between two cars is a workout. If your family regularly swaps children between vehicles, consider buying two seats or choosing a lighter convertible.

## Our Verdict

For families with one car and one child, the 4Ever DLX eliminates seat-buying anxiety entirely. The investment pays off after year 2, when you'd otherwise be shopping for a new seat.

## Safety Standards & Certifications

Car seats sold in the United States must comply with **FMVSS 213** (Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard No. 213), which governs dynamic crash performance, harness strength, and flammability. Look for **JPMA certification** as an additional quality marker. All infant car seats are also tested against **ASTM F2050**. The safest installation method is always LATCH or a correctly tightened seat belt — never both simultaneously unless the manufacturer explicitly permits it.

For the **Graco 4Ever DLX** specifically: 10-position adjustable headrest with Simply Safe Adjust harness, which is independently verified. Our 8.8/10 score factors in full safety compliance.
