The **Chicco KeyFit 35** has been America's best-selling infant car seat for years. The reason is simple: it's the easiest infant seat to install correctly —  a critical safety factor that many parents underestimate.

## Installation: The Best in Class

Car seat installation errors are extremely common and dangerous. The KeyFit 35 solves this with a bubble level indicator (shows correct recline angle), a SuperCinch LATCH tightener, and audible click indicators. In our tests, we installed it correctly on the first try in 4 different vehicles.

## Safety Ratings

The KeyFit 35 has consistently earned top safety ratings from the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA). The steel frame and side-impact foam provide excellent protection.

## Ease of Use

The one-hand release button clicks in and out of the base with zero effort. At 9.4 lbs, it's manageable even when carrying a sleeping baby. The carry handle has multiple positions for ergonomic carrying.

## Verdict

For first-time parents who want the highest confidence in correct installation, the Chicco KeyFit 35 is the first choice. It's not the cheapest, but installation errors are the leading cause of car seat failures —  and this seat virtually eliminates that risk.
