The **Evenflo EveryStage DLX** exists to answer a simple question: do I have to spend $400+ on a car seat to keep my child safe? The answer is no — and the EveryStage DLX proves it.

## Safety Without the Premium Price

Every car seat sold in the United States must pass rigorous federal safety standards. The EveryStage DLX passes those standards with room to spare. NHTSA's ease-of-use ratings are consistently positive, and independent crash test performance is solid.

What you give up at $180 vs $400 is build quality, materials, ease of installation, and premium features — not the fundamental crash protection that matters most.

## Rear-Facing to 40 lbs

The EveryStage DLX rear-faces to 40 lbs — below the Nuna RAVA's 50 lbs but meeting the AAP's extended rear-facing recommendations for most children through age 3. For families where budget is the primary consideration, this is a genuine extended rear-facing seat at an accessible price.

## Three Configurations

**Rear-facing:** 4–40 lbs with 5-point harness
**Forward-facing:** 22–65 lbs with 5-point harness  
**Booster mode:** 40–120 lbs as high-back booster

Machine-washable seat pad and included cup holders make daily use practical.

## Best For

Budget-conscious families, grandparents who need a spare seat, families with three+ children who need multiple seats, and families in warmer climates where cars get hot and premium fabric is less important.
