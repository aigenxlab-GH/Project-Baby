The **Joovy Caboose Ultralight Graphite** addresses the double stroller dilemma: you need to carry two children, but traditional double strollers are either enormous, impossibly heavy, or both.

## The Sit-and-Stand Design

The Caboose uses a tandem layout where the front child sits normally and the rear child can either sit in a jump seat or stand on a platform behind the front seat. This "caboose" configuration keeps the stroller narrow enough to fit through standard doorways — at 25.5 inches, it's narrower than most single strollers with large frames.

## 26 Pounds Changes Everything

The "Ultralight" name earns its claim. At 26 lbs empty, the Caboose is 8–15 lbs lighter than competing double strollers. When you're already managing two children, that weight difference matters enormously for loading into car trunks, navigating stairs, and pushing over varied terrain.

## Infant + Toddler Configuration

The most common Caboose configuration: infant car seat in the front position (with adapter), toddler standing or sitting in the rear. This covers the ages from newborn through 45 lbs in the rear — the exact window that causes most double stroller purchases.

## Car Seat Compatibility

The Joovy Universal Car Seat Adapter (sold separately) is compatible with most major infant car seat brands. Check Joovy's compatibility list for your specific seat model.

## The Trade-offs

The rear standing position adds length to the overall stroller footprint when a child is standing. Pushing a loaded double stroller on hills is harder than a single stroller regardless of weight savings. And the ride quality, while acceptable, doesn't match premium single strollers.


## Who It's For

The **Joovy Caboose Ultralight Graphite** is the right choice when choosing a primary stroller if:

**Think twice if:** Standing platform can make handling heavier when loaded.

## Safety Standards & Certifications

Strollers must meet **ASTM F833**, which covers stability, braking, structural integrity, and restraint systems. JPMA-certified strollers carry an independent quality verification seal. Always use the provided harness — stroller injuries are most commonly caused by falls from an unrestrained child.

For the **Joovy Caboose Ultralight Graphite** specifically: only 26 lbs — lightest full-size double stroller available, which is independently verified. Our 8.5/10 score factors in full safety compliance.
