The **Chicco Bravo Trio Travel System** is the go-to choice for parents who want a proven, easy-to-use travel system without the premium stroller price. The included KeyFit 30 is among the most trusted infant car seats in the world.

## The KeyFit 30 Car Seat

Chicco's KeyFit 30 has been the #1 selling infant car seat in America for multiple consecutive years — for good reason. Installation is nearly foolproof: a spring-loaded leveling indicator shows correct angle, and the LATCH connection clicks securely. NHTSA misuse rates are consistently among the lowest available.

## One-Hand Fold That Actually Works

Many strollers advertise "one-hand fold" but require two hands to actually manage. The Bravo folds with a single pull of the handle and stands independently — meaning you don't need to set baby down or hold the stroller with your knee while fumbling. For parents managing an infant and a stroller simultaneously, this is genuinely valuable.

## Lightweight for a Travel System

At 25 lbs, the Bravo is noticeably lighter than the Graco Modes Pramette (28 lbs) or many other travel systems. This matters when loading into car trunks dozens of times per week.

## The Reversible Seat

The toddler seat reverses to face either direction — parent-facing for younger babies who want to see your face, world-facing for older babies who want to see everything else. No tools required.

## What's Missing

The Bravo doesn't offer a flat pram mode, which some newborn experts recommend. The storage basket is partially blocked when the infant seat is installed. And at 30 lbs, the KeyFit's weight limit is lower than the Graco KeyFit 35 included in the Pramette system.
