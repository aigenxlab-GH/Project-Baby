The **Doona Infant Car Seat & Stroller** is one of the most genuinely innovative baby products of the past decade — a car seat that deploys into a rolling stroller in under 5 seconds, without removing the baby.

## The Core Innovation

Every parent knows the struggle: you park, you get out, you carefully transfer a sleeping newborn from car seat to stroller, and they wake up. The Doona eliminates this entirely. Baby stays in the seat — which stays buckled — as you flip down the frame and the wheels lock into place. Drive to the farmers' market, park, click out the Doona, and you're rolling in 5 seconds with a sleeping baby still asleep.

## Who It's For

The Doona makes the most sense for:
- Urban parents who drive occasionally but walk a lot
- Frequent travelers (it fits in aircraft overhead bins when folded)
- Parents who dread the transfer of a sleeping newborn
- Single parents managing infant transport solo

## The Trade-offs

The Doona stroller doesn't have a large basket, canopy (sold separately), or the smooth ride of a dedicated full-size stroller. It's a transitional, newborn-phase tool. Once your baby outgrows the infant seat (at 35 lbs or about 12–18 months), you'll need to transition to a convertible car seat and a separate toddler stroller anyway.

At $550, it's one of the most expensive infant seats available. But for travel-heavy families, the convenience is remarkable.

## Safety Record

The Doona has been independently crash-tested and meets all US safety standards. It's FAA-approved for use in airplane seats. The stroller frame mechanism doesn't compromise the seat's structural integrity.
