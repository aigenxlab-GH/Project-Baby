The **Graco Modes Pramette Travel System** is the travel system we recommend to parents who want the best value combination of safety, versatility, and price — without jumping to $1,000+ premium options.

## The KeyFit 35 Car Seat

The included Graco KeyFit 35 is genuinely one of the best infant car seats available. The SnugLock belt installation locks with a single click and is nearly impossible to install incorrectly — NHTSA misuse rates for the KeyFit are among the lowest of any infant seat. The extended weight limit (35 lbs) means it outlasts most competing infant seats.

## What Pramette Mode Adds

Most travel systems include a stroller that holds the infant car seat in a forward-facing bucket position. The Pramette goes further: the stroller seat itself reclines to a near-flat pram position for newborns who can't yet safely sit upright for extended periods. This is a significant advantage for newborn development and is a feature typically found only on premium strollers.

## Three Stroller Modes

1. **Pramette mode:** Flat recline, parent-facing for newborns
2. **Infant seat mode:** Snap in the KeyFit 35 for easy transitions
3. **Toddler mode:** Upright seat for growing babies, reversible direction

## What You Sacrifice for the Price

At 28 lbs, the Pramette is heavy — noticeably more so than premium alternatives. The maneuverability on tight sidewalks doesn't match UPPAbaby or Bugaboo. If you live in a dense city and walk miles daily, consider the extra investment in a lighter frame.
