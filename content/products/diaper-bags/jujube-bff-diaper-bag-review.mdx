The **JuJuBe BFF** commands a $200 price tag — and for parents who do the math, it actually represents excellent value. Here's why.

## Machine Washable: The Feature That Changes Everything

Every parent who owns a diaper bag has experienced the horror: a sippy cup leaks, a diaper fails catastrophically, or a forgotten banana turns to liquid in the bottom pocket. With any normal bag, that's a spot-cleaning emergency. With the JuJuBe BFF, you unzip everything, throw the entire bag in the washing machine, and it comes out looking new.

This isn't a parlor trick — the bag is specifically engineered with sealed zippers, rust-resistant hardware, and reinforced seams to survive repeated machine washing over years.

## Antimicrobial Lining

The AgION silver-based treatment inside every pocket inhibits bacteria and mold growth at the microscopic level. This means dried formula, spilled milk, and diaper odors don't become permanent bag residents. It's the reason JuJuBe bags smell fresh even after years of use.

## The 13-Pocket Organization

A dedicated mommy pocket (fits a 15-inch laptop), two insulated bottle pockets, a crumb-catcher bottom panel, internal key clip, and a wipeable changing mat. The BFF converts between backpack, shoulder bag, and crossbody — useful for different outings.

## The Premium Investment

At $200, the JuJuBe BFF is 1.5–2x the price of competitors. But with a lifetime guarantee and the washability that prevents replacement, it's often cheaper long-term than buying two or three cheaper bags over three years.
