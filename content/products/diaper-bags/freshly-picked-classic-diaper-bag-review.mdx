The **Freshly Picked Classic Diaper Bag** was founded on a simple premise: parents shouldn't have to choose between fashion and function. After years of testing every diaper bag available, we can say Freshly Picked delivers on that promise better than any competitor.

## Design That Transcends "Diaper Bag"

Most diaper bags — even good ones like the Skip Hop Forma — have a distinctly utilitarian look. The Freshly Picked looks like a high-end handbag or designer backpack. The structured vegan leather exterior, minimal hardware, and clean lines make it appropriate for restaurants, work meetings, and weddings, not just the playground.

## Vegan Leather: Better Than It Sounds

PU vegan leather has a reputation for peeling over time, but Freshly Picked uses a notably thicker material than competitors. Owners consistently report 3+ years of daily use without peeling, cracking, or fading. It wipes clean from formula spills, mud, and food — important for a bag that spends time on changing room floors.

## Organization in 10 Pockets

The pockets are thoughtfully arranged: two insulated bottle pockets, a front zip pocket for on-the-go access, a back stroller attachment pocket, a wipeable main compartment, and internal organization. The changing mat stores in the base, keeping the main compartment free.

## The Style Trade-off

The premium look costs extra weight — about 2.2 lbs empty, heavier than fabric alternatives. And at $180, it's not for budget-conscious shoppers. But for parents who will use this bag daily for 2–3 years, the per-day cost is reasonable.
